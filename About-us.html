About PathCA

A practice platform born from the same late-night study sessions, revision anxiety, and exam pressure you experience

PathCA is not another ed-tech startup built in a boardroom. It is an online CA Foundation practice platform designed by someone currently living through the same preparation cycle as you. No venture capital. No marketing team. Just a student who realized that watching strategy videos doesn't build exam skills-practice does.

The Problem We Actually Faced

It started with the same routine you probably know too well. Open YouTube for "CA Foundation strategy." Watch three toppers explain their routines. Feel inspired. Close the laptop. Realize you've consumed three hours of content and solved zero questions.

The next day, repeat. The dopamine of planning feels like progress. But the books remain unopened. The mock tests remain untouched. And the exam date keeps approaching.

We were spending more time watching people study than actually studying. The mobile phone was a distraction device, not a learning tool.

- Kanhaiya Chaudhary, Founder

What PathCA Actually Does

PathCA transforms your mobile phone from a distraction into a practice weapon. It is designed for short, intense practice sessions-the kind you can do while commuting, during lunch breaks, or in those 20-minute gaps between classes.

The Core Philosophy

Practice before the exam, not just study. Theory without execution is just entertainment. PathCA focuses on execution.

The platform offers chapter-wise MCQ practice aligned with the CA Foundation syllabus, RTP and MTP-based test patterns that mirror actual exam conditions, and instant evaluation that tells you exactly where you stand-no waiting, no ambiguity.

  • Practice CA Foundation MCQs chapter-wise with immediate feedback
  • Revise using RTP and MTP pattern questions that actually appear in exams
  • Test yourself in timed, exam-like conditions to build pressure tolerance
  • Track performance metrics that matter: accuracy, speed, and consistency
  • Identify weak areas through pattern recognition, not guesswork

Built by a Student, Not a Corporation

Built by an 18-year-old CA Foundation Student

PathCA is not built by a large ed-tech company with million-dollar funding. It is designed and developed by someone currently preparing for the CA Foundation exam-someone who understands the real pressure of ICAI's pattern, the anxiety of RTP releases, and the panic of last-minute revision.

Every feature is shaped from practical student experience. Every button placement is tested during actual study sessions. Every question format is validated against past exam papers.

My name is Kanhaiya Chaudhary. I built PathCA initially as a personal revision tool because I couldn't find a platform that let me practice MCQs quickly without the bloat of video lectures and theory dumps. When classmates started asking to use it, I realized this wasn't just my problem.

Why PathCA Is Different

Traditional Platforms

  • 3-hour video lectures that kill momentum

  • Generic content not specific to CA Foundation
  • Delayed feedback loops (wait for evaluation)
  • Desktop-first design that ignores mobile users

✓ PathCA Approach

  • Quick MCQ practice in 10-15 minute bursts

Mission: Build the Daily Practice Habit

The mission is simple but difficult: help CA Foundation students build a daily practice habit using a platform that respects their time and attention.

  • Encourage regular MCQ practice even on "low motivation" days
  • Reduce last-minute exam pressure through distributed practice
  • Make revision measurable so students can't lie to themselves
  • Support exam-oriented preparation, not theory accumulation

You don't need perfect study days. You need consistent practice. Even 20 MCQs daily compounds into thousands before the exam.

Vision: The Future of PathCA

PathCA aims to grow into a complete mobile-first practice ecosystem for CA Foundation students. Not a coaching replacement. Not a theory database. A performance gym where you train your exam execution skills daily.
