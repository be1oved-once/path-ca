Q: What is the study of how people allocate scarce resources called?
A: Sociology
B: Economics

D: Politics
ANSWER: B
Q: What is the term for a continuous rise in the general price level of goods and services?
A: Inflation
B: Deflation

A: Recession
B: Deflation
